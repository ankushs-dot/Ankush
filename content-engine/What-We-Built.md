# Pandit ji Content Engine — What We Built

## In one line
A private tool that automatically creates ready-to-film Reel ideas for the Pandit ji page — each with a hook, a 25-second script, a caption, and hashtags, all in Pandit ji's Hinglish style.

## Why it matters
Instead of spending hours every day thinking of Reel ideas and writing scripts, we click one button and get a full batch of fresh, on-brand ideas based on what is actually trending today.

## How it works — a team of 4 AI helpers
The tool runs like a 4-step assembly line:

1. **Trend Scraper** — scans YouTube and finds the astrology / numerology / horoscope videos getting the most views right now.
2. **Viral Analyzer** — looks at the real numbers (views, likes, comments) and picks the true winners — the videos beating everything else.
3. **Script Writer** — writes a brand-new 25-second script in Pandit ji's exact Hinglish voice, based on those winning patterns.
4. **Hook + Caption Maker** — adds the scroll-stopping opening line, the caption, and the hashtags.

## What you see on screen
- **Topic Library tab** — all 90 of our existing calendar scripts, searchable by sign, number, or theme. Copy any one instantly.
- **Generate New (AI) tab** — pick a category, click "Run engine," and the AI writes fresh scripts. A green panel shows the real trend data it used (which videos it scraped, the top performer, and rising Google searches) — proof it is using live data, not guesswork.

## What makes it "real"
The ideas are not random. They are built from:
- **Real YouTube trend data** (last 60 days, India).
- **Real Google search trends** (what people are searching this week).
- **Our own past scripts**, so the voice always sounds like Pandit ji.

## Cost

- The trend data (YouTube + Google) is free.
